Our Story

Founded in 2010, Gourmet3D brings a revolutionary approach to fine dining by combining culinary excellence with immersive 3D experiences.

Our chef, Michel LeGrand, trained under some of the world's most renowned culinary masters before developing his unique vision of multi-dimensional dining.

Every dish at Gourmet3D is designed to engage all your senses, creating memories that linger long after the last bite.
